Designing for human beings:
"How hard can it be?" - they said.

Around my 20th birthday, I realized that I wanted my crayons back so I started to draw again, but not on paper this time.

The result of combining it with research, learning and dedication was my top accomplishment of that time. In time, I also developed an affinity towards human psychology, which served me as a tool in understanding the predictability of human behaviour.

Thus I came to the realization that by choosing a career path in User Experience, User Interface and User Interaction Design, I could combine two of the things I love to observe and learn about.
