For movie lovers

Cinemap is a concept designed for the cinephiles out there. It brings close together movie ratings, trailers and booking the ticket for the premiere at the nearest cinema.

Search - Choose - Book. These are the three steps meant to transform going to the cinema into a pleasure, getting rid of waiting in line for the ticket or the repeated "What should we watch?".

Designed to find the nearest Cinema, and with an easy 2 step booking process, Cinemap helps the user enjoy the best movies without the unnecessary pain.
