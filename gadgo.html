A fun and unforgivable traveling experience.

The following concept is meant to bring a fun and unforgivable traveling experience. Gadgo is a gamified traveling app that is supposed to offer the user the possibility to choose from various random itineraries in a city, in order to discover traditional places and to earn coins while doing it, coins that can be used as discounts in specific locations.
Features: Memories Diary, Customized Itineraries, Public Transport Ticket in-App purchase...
