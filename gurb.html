The easy path to combining busy days with healthy eating.

Designed for people preoccupied by a healthy lifestyle, Gurb's main purpose is to deliver high quality food in just a few clicks, diminishing the time the user spends searching for the perfect lunch.

The design thinking behind this concept started with finding out the main pains users experience when ordering food online without having too much time to decide: bothering ads with lots of different suggestions - that make the decision taking longer and more complicated, cluttered designs that diminish the user's focus.

I concluded that an easy, clean and intuitive interface would help the customer access the desired content faster and a profile based pre-filled with the user's preferences would make the suggestions more effective and useful.
